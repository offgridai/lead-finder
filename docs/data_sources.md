# Candidate Data Sources

## Discovery

- Google Places API or licensed local business data
- Campground and RV directories
- Tourism bureau and chamber sites
- Hipcamp / campsite directories where permitted
- Manual seed lists from domain experts

## Ownership and property

- Hocking County assessor / GIS
- County recorder records
- Ohio Secretary of State business search
- Business license records where available
- Permit records where available

## Activity

- Business website
- Booking engine
- Review platforms via approved APIs or licensed data
- Social profiles
- Public permit activity
- Public photos and site maps

## Financial proxies

- Site count / cabin count / unit capacity
- Published rates
- Booking calendar availability where permitted
- Review velocity
- Seasonality assumptions
- Industry operating-margin benchmarks

## Ethics and compliance

- Use public or licensed business data.
- Respect robots.txt and terms of service.
- Do not infer or store unnecessary personal sensitive information.
- Keep outreach respectful and evidence-based.
